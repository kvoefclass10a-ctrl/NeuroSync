================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Issue: Content not displaying properly on mobile
Solution: Check for fixed widths, ensure containers have width: 100%

Issue: Text is too small or too large
Solution: Check font-size media queries in mobile-optimizations.css

Issue: Buttons are hard to click on mobile
Solution: Ensure buttons have min-height: 44px and min-width: 44px

Issue: Horizontal scroll on mobile
Solution: Look for elements exceeding 100vw or fixed widths

Issue: Images not scaling properly
Solution: Ensure images have max-width: 100% and height: auto

Issue: Forms not working well on mobile
Solution: Ensure input font-size is 16px to prevent iOS auto-zoom
